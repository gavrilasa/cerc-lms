# CERC Learning Management System

A learning platform designed for **CERC (Computer Engineering Research Club)** to manage courses, track student progress, and facilitate learning across different divisions.

---

## What is CERC LMS?

CERC LMS is an internal learning platform that allows members to:

- **Browse and enroll in courses** organized by their division (Software, Embedded, Multimedia, Networking)
- **Watch video lessons** and track their learning progress
- **Complete curriculum paths** designed for their specific division
- **Submit assignments and projects** for mentor review
- **Compete on leaderboards** based on points earned from completed work

---

## User Roles

| Role       | Access                                                    |
| ---------- | --------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Guest**  | Can register and wait for approval                        |
| **Member** | Access to dashboard, courses, and submissions             |
| **Mentor** | Can review submissions and manage users in their division |
| **Admin**  | Full access to manage courses, curricula, and all users   |

---

## How to Use

### For Members

1. **Sign up** with your account and wait for admin approval
2. **Select a curriculum** based on your division
3. **Browse available courses** from your dashboard
4. **Enroll in courses** and start learning
5. **Mark lessons as complete** as you progress
6. **Submit tasks/projects** to earn points
7. **Track your rank** on the leaderboard

### For Mentors

1. **Access the admin panel** to review pending submissions
2. **Grade submissions** and provide feedback
3. **View users** in your assigned division
4. **Monitor leaderboard** standings

### For Admins

1. **Create and manage courses** with chapters and lessons
2. **Design curricula** for each division
3. **Approve or reject** user registrations
4. **Manage all users** and their roles
5. **View system logs** for audit purposes
